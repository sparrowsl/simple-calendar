Sierra Leone Association for Developers

If you believe in yourself anything is possible. Decision defines Destiny

SLAD is a non-profit organization with the aim of helping Sierra Leoneans that are interested in tech by pairing people with the same level of skill and bringing experienced mentors in different fields to help in Leading, Organizing Hackatons, and Competitions, among developers and help promote the technology sector in Sierra Leone.

Most people are interested in technology but they lack the resources to learn, the road map in a specific area of study, the community of other learners or the idea of what job is available, and most times find it hard to connect with others that are also interested/learning about the vast technology skills (especially students and self taughts). Finding people that live in the same town scares most learners off and kills their drive and passion for tech. They will have no option but to choose another field of study instead of following their dream. Our aim is to bring all these people together, provide resources, road maps, guidance and the help they need to grow into a good IT engineer.

Join us in our journey to make Sierra Leone a better place! and help increase the knowledge about the benefits, advantages and the ease IT will bring to our nation.
