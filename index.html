Sierra Leone Association for Developers

If you believe in yourself anything is possible. Decision defines Destiny

SLAD is a non profit organization with the aim of helping Sierra Leoneans interested in tech by pairing like-minded people with the same level of skill, bringing experience mentors to help in Leading, Organizing Hackatons, and Competitions, among developers and help to motivate others that are interested in tech.

Most people are passionate about the tech world, but lack resources, fear of the job role or even a glimpse of other students that are also interested and living in the same country/town scares most people off and demotivates then, so they had no option but to choose another field. Our aim is to bring all those people together and help the growth, benefits and advantages of IT in Sierra Leone.

Join us in our journey and help make Sierra Leone a better place! and help increase the knowledge about IT in our nation.
