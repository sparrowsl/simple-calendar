Sierra Leone Association for Developers

If you believe in yourself anything is possible because Decision defines Destiny

SLAD is a non profit organization with the aim of helping Sierra Leoneans that are interested in tech by pairing people with the same level of skill and bringing experienced mentors in different fields to help in Leading young developers, Organizing Hackatons, Organize Bootcamps and Competitions among developers and help promote the technology sector in Sierra Leone.

Join us in our journey to make Sierra Leone a better place and help spread the knowledge about the benefits, advantages and the ease Technology will bring to our nation.
