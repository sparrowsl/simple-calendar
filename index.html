Sierra Leone Association for Developers

If you believe in yourself anything is possible because Decision defines Destiny

SLAD is a non profit organization with the aim of helping Sierra Leoneans that are passionate about tech. We help by pairing people with the same level of skill to ease the learning process and bringing experienced mentors/people in different fields to help in Leading and training young developers, Organizing Hackatons, Bootcamps and Competitions among developers to help promote the technology sector in Sierra Leone.

Join us in our journey to make Sierra Leone a better place.
